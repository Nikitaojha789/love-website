From My Soul

A Letter I Wrote a Hundred Times

❝
❞

My love,

I won't pretend that everything was perfect. We fought — really fought — and for a moment, the silence between us felt heavier than anything I'd ever carried. I missed you in a way that hurt, even when you were the reason for the hurt.

But here's what I learned in that one month apart: I am not whole without you. Not because I can't survive alone — I can. But because every beautiful thing I see, every song that plays, every quiet evening — they all whisper your name.

We fought. We stayed.
We healed. We chose each other again.
And that — that choosing — is the most beautiful thing we've ever done.

"The most addictive fragrance was never in a bottle."

It was you. It has always been you. The way you laugh, the way you exist, the way you make ordinary moments feel like something worth remembering forever. I am addicted to you — to us — to this love that refuses to give up.

So here I am. Not perfect. Not without flaws. But completely, irrevocably yours. I am choosing you today, tomorrow, and every single day after that.

With every piece of me,
— Yours, Always 💕
